Manipulation · SmolVLA
smolvla_so101_embodied_v1 · real-robot rollouts
fork partial lego partial open-loop beats baselines on train frames

Open-loop replay on frames from so101_embodied_tasks_v1 shows the checkpoint on the Hub is not underfit — predicted actions beat no-op and mean-action baselines. On the physical SO-101 the policy still undershoots grasps and is sensitive to object displacement.

Main failure mode: undershoot on reach; small shifts in lego/fork position break the motion. Weights at fbarbosa1/smolvla_so101_embodied_v1.

Manipulation · π₀.5
pi05_so101_embodied_v1 · real-robot rollouts
fork partial–success lego partial–success open-loop learned on train frames

Like SmolVLA, open-loop diagnostics on the Hugging Face checkpoint look healthy — the gap is closed-loop deployment. π₀.5 is language-conditioned: a wrong or ambiguous prompt (or both tasks' objects left on the table) produces task confusion and approach from the wrong direction.

Main failure mode: multi-task bleed when fork and lego/cup cues coexist; YOLO-augmented prompts help until a detector miss sends bad spatial text. Weights at fbarbosa1/pi05_so101_embodied_v1, training data at so101_embodied_tasks_v1.
